Welcome to Penmorva

Where the day starts
with the sound of surf.

Set high on the headland with uninterrupted views across the bay, Penmorva blends the calm of a country home with the wildness of the Cornish coast. Stone floors, a wood-burner, and big windows that frame the sea.

Wake to the light off the water, walk the coast path before breakfast, and come home to a kitchen made for long, slow dinners. It sleeps six in comfort, and the dog is welcome too.
